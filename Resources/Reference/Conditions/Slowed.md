# Slowed

You have fewer actions. Slowed always includes a value. When you regain your actions, reduce the number of actions regained by your slowed value. Because you regain actions at the start of your turn, you don't immediately lose actions if you becomes slowed during your turn.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 446 <sup>2.0</sup>*
