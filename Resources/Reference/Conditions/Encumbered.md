# Encumbered

You are carrying more weight than you can manage. While you're encumbered, you're [[Clumsy|clumsy 1]] and take a 10-foot penalty to all your Speeds. As with all penalties to your Speed, this can't reduce your Speed below 5 feet.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 443 <sup>2.0</sup>*
