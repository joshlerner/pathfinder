# Climb `pf2:1`

==[[Move]]==

**Requirements** You have two hands free.
___
You attempt an [[Athletics]] check to move a maximum distance of 5 feet up, down, or across an incline. You're [[off-guard]] while climbing unless you have a climb Speed. The GM determines the DC based on the nature of the incline and environmental circumstances; you might get an automatic critical success on an incline that's trivial to climb. If your land Speed is 40 feet or highter, increase the maximum distance by 5 feet for every 20 feet of Speed above 20 feet.

**Critical Success** You move along the incline, increasing the maximum distance by 5 feet.
**Success** You move along the incline.
**Critical Failure** You fall, If you began the climb on stable ground, you fall and land [[prone]].

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 234 <sup>2.0</sup>*
