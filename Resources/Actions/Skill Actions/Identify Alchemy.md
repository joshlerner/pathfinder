# Identify Alchemy

==[[Concentrate]]== ==[[Exploration]]== ==[[Secret]]==

**Requirements** You are holding or wearing an alchemist's toolkit.
___
You can identify the nature or an [[alchemical]] item with 10 minutes of testing using your alchemist's toolkit. If your attempt is interrupted in any way, you must start over.

**Success** You identify the item and the means of activating it.
**Failure** You fail to identify the item but can try again.
**Critical Failure** You misidentify the item as another item of the GM's choice.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 237 <sup>2.0</sup>*
