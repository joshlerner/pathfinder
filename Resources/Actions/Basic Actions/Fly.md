# Fly `pf2:1`

==[[Move]]==

**Requirements** You must have a fly Speed.
___
You move through the air up to you fly Speed. Moving upward uses the rules for moving through difficult terrain. You can move straight down 10 feet for every 5 feet of movement you spend. If you Fly to the ground, you don't take falling damage. You can use an action to Fly 0 feet to hover in place. If you're airborne at the end of your turn and didn't use a Fly action this round, you fall.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 419 <sup>2.0</sup>*
