# Arrest a Fall `pf2:r`

**Trigger** You fall.
**Requirements** You have a fly Speed.
___
You attempt you choice of an [[Acrobatics]] check or Reflex save to slow your fall. The DC is typically 15, but it might be higher due to air turbulence or other circumstances.

**Success** You take no damage from the fall.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 418 <sup>2.0</sup>*
