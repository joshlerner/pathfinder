# Interact `pf2:1`

==[[Manipulate]]==
___
You use your hand or hands to manipulate an object or the terrain. You can grab an unattended or stored object, draw a weapon, swap a held item for another, open a door, or achieve a similar effect. On rare occasions, you might have to attempt a skill to determine if your Interact action was successful.

**Source** *Pathfinder Player Core pg. 416 <sup>2.0</sup>*
