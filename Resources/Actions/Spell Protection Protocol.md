# Spell Protection Protocol `pf2:1`

==[[Arcane]]== ==[[Manipulate]]== ==[[Technomancer]]==

**Prerequisites** FORTRUN or ServoShell programming language
___
You run an app that temporarily diverts incoming spell energy. You or an ally within 30 feet gaina 5-foot aura that grants the target and allies within the aura a +1 status bonus to saving throws against spells and effects with the magic trait. Increase this bonus to +3 against spells you cast. The aura lasts until the end of your next turn, and you can Sustain it, to a maximum duration of 3 rounds.

**Source** *Starfinder Tech Class pg. 27*
