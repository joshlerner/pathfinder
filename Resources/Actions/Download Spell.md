# Download Spell `pf2:0`

==[[Concentrate]]== ==[[Technomancer]]==

**Frequency** once every 10 minutes
___
You overwrite one of your prepared spells with one of the spells in your spell cache of the same rank or lower. Select one of your prepared non-cantrip spells that has not been expended. Thsi spell slot is emptied and one of your cached spells is instantly prepared in its place, heightened to the slot it fills.

**Source** *Starfinder Tech Class pg. 22*
